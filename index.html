I am a doctoral student in Education with emphases in Cognitive Science and Quantitative Methods in the Social Sciences at UC Santa Barbara.

My research interests include using VR for literacy instruction and second language learning, educational measurement, and data science. I received a B.A. in Linguistics from UC Riverside and an M.A. in Linguistics with a specialization in Language and Mind from California State University, Fullerton. Currently, I am working as a Linguistic Analyst and Program Task Designer for the Virtual Reality Project as part of the Community-Based Literacies (CBL) Initiative.

My advisors are Dr. Andrew Maul and Dr. Diana Arya.
