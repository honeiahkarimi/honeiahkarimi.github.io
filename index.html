I am a doctoral student in Education with an emphasis in Quantitative Methods in the Social Sciences at UC Santa Barbara.

My research interests include data science, educational measurement, and using Virtual Reality for language learning. I received a B.A. in Linguistics from UC Riverside and an M.A. in Linguistics from California State University, Fullerton. Currently, I am working as a Linguistic Specialist and Program Task Designer for the Virtual Reality Project as part of the Community-Based Literacies (CBL) Initiative.
